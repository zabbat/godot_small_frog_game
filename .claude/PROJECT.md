# Small Frog Game

A small-scale 3rd person 3D mini RPG built in Godot 4.6.1.

## Overview
A compact RPG where the player explores a world with villages, fights monsters, interacts with NPCs, and levels up their character.

## Core Features
- **3rd Person 3D** camera and movement
- **Combat** — fight monsters in the world
- **Leveling** — gain experience and level up
- **Equipment** — find and equip gear
- **Villages** — visit towns with NPCs
- **NPCs & Dialogs** — talk to characters with a dialog system
- **Monsters** — enemies with varying difficulty
